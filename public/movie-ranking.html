The Best Movies According to Mike

My top of 3 movies of all-time

The green book

This movie shows the problem of racism in the USA during the 1960s. The movie shows how racism is not just a choice, but more like a tradition

Hachiko

This movie shows the unconditional love an animal can have for its owner.

Gattaca

This movie shows that if you really want to achieve a goal no matter the life's challenges puts in your way if you truly want to do it, you will.
